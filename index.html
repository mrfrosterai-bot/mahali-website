Our Standard

Halal Is
the Beginning

Quality you can taste. Ethics you can trust.

Halal isn't just a certification — it's a philosophy of care. From how animals are raised to how they're processed, every step reflects a commitment to doing things right.

We partner exclusively with producers who go beyond the minimum. Pasture-raised. Humanely handled. Traceable from farm to your front door.

Ethical Sourcing

Every producer in our network is verified for humane animal treatment, sustainable practices, and full halal compliance.

Cultural Celebration

Charcuterie has always existed in halal cuisines — from Turkish pastırma to Moroccan khlii. We're bringing those traditions to the modern table.

Radical Transparency

Know your farmer. Know your butcher. Every item in your box comes with its origin story and certifications.

We didn't start Mahali to sell meat. We started it because our families deserved the same charcuterie experience everyone else has — without compromise.

— The Mahali Team
